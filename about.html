About Us

Independent travel journalism since 2011.

Our Mission

When in Roam publishes travel stories that go beyond the standard tourist experience. We send writers to places that don't get much coverage and ask them to spend enough time there to actually understand what they're writing about. We don't accept sponsored trips or paid placements — our writers pay their own way, which means they're free to write honestly.
